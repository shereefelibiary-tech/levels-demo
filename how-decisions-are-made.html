Coronary Calcium (CAC)

CAC is used only as a tie-breaker in preference-sensitive risk zones. It is obtained only if the result would delay therapy (CAC = 0) or prompt initiation or intensification (CAC > 0).

This framework aligns with the intent of ACC/AHA and ESC prevention guidance while making clinical uncertainty explicit and operational.
